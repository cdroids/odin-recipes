Crispy Rice Salad>

This is my favorite crispy rice recipe. The base is a air fried rice which gets mixed into herbs and a really delicous salad dressing. To spice this up or add some protein, you can cook salmon. To see the salmon recipe clickhere.

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1.2 inch knob of ginger
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons of avocado oil
  • 1/4 cup of lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • optional1/2 tablespoon of chili crisp

Crispy Rice Salad

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 2 teaspoons red curry paste
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • half an English cucumber
  • 1 shallotsliced thinly
  • 1 bunch cilantro, roughly cut
  • 1 bunch mint
    • 1/2 cup peanuts
  • chili crispsoptional
